Building an Interface - Junior Mathematical Conference, Miskolc, 1999

In 1999, I participated in the Junior Mathematical Conference in Miskolc, Hungary, where I presented a device serving as an I/O interface between a PC and other electronical devices. The written version of the presentation was published in the periodical Pi.
