CoverShow, 2006

CoverShow was a large-scale musical production written and directed by Balázs Róbert Suda, which premiered at the Belvárosi Theatre in Budapest, Hungary. Employer: EXAU Ltd. The design project consisted of:

  • Logo design for the production (see gallery below)
  • Webdesign and programming for a site with a hierarchical menu system but with static pages: link
  • Flyer and poster design (see gallery below)
  • Ad design for various magazines and publications
  • Animated and static web banners
  • A short video clip to be played on screens in the theatre (see below)
